## Mental Health Recommendations

### Daily Mental Health Maintenance

**Daily Practices**:
1. **Regular Schedule**: Maintain stable sleep-wake cycles
2. **Physical Activity**: At least 30 minutes of exercise daily
3. **Healthy Diet**: Balanced nutrition, limit caffeine and alcohol
4. **Social Connection**: Maintain important relationships
5. **Mindfulness Practice**: 10-15 minutes of meditation or relaxation daily
6. **Self-Compassion**: Set aside time for yourself

**Stress Prevention**:
- Recognize stress signals
- Set reasonable expectations
- Learn to say "no"
- Prioritize important tasks
- Regular rest and recovery

### Emotion Regulation Strategies

**Mood Enhancement**:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Basic Mental Health Knowledge

**Q: What is mental health?**
A: Mental health includes:
- Emotional well-being
- Psychological functioning
- Social well-being
- Ability to cope with life stresses
- Work ability
- Contribution to community

**Q: How to identify mental health problems?**
A: Watch for these signals:
- Persistent sadness or low mood
- Excessive anxiety or worry
- Social withdrawal
- Changes in sleep or appetite
- Fatigue or lack of energy
- Difficulty concentrating
- Feelings of worthlessness or guilt
- Physical symptoms without medical cause

### Coping Strategies

**Q: How to cope with stress?**
A: You can try:
1. Identify stressors
2. Change stressful environment
3. Learn relaxation techniques
4. Seek social support
5. Maintain healthy lifestyle
6. Seek professional help

**Q: How to improve mood?**
A: You can try:
1. Regular exercise
2. Adequate sleep
3. Healthy diet
4. Mindfulness practice
5. Social activities
6. Pursue hobbies
7. Help others

### When to Seek Professional Help

**Q: When should I seek professional help?**
A: Seek help when:
- Symptoms persist for more than 2 weeks
- Affect daily functioning
- Have thoughts of self-harm or harming others
- Using substances to cope
- Friends/family express concern
- Self-coping strategies are ineffective

**Q: How to find mental health professionals?**
A: You can find through:
1. Primary care physician referral
2. Health insurance provider list
3. Mental health hotlines
4. Online therapy platforms
5. Community mental health centers
6. Referrals and word of mouth

## Mental Health and Chronic Diseases

### Hypertension

**Psychological Factors and Blood Pressure**:
- Chronic stress can raise blood pressure
- Anxiety and depression affect blood pressure control
- Mental health affects medication adherence

**Improvement Recommendations**:
- Learn stress management
- Seek mental support
- Improve sleep quality
- Regular relaxation practice

### Diabetes

**Psychology and Blood Sugar**:
- Stress affects blood sugar levels
- Diabetes distress is common
- Depression is more common in diabetics

**Improvement Recommendations**:
- Accept diabetes as a chronic condition
- Seek diabetes support groups
- Manage diabetes distress
- Seek psychological help if needed

### Heart Disease

**Psychology and Heart**:
- Depression increases heart disease risk
- Stress affects heart health
- Anxiety is associated with heart symptoms

**Improvement Recommendations**:
- Manage stress levels
- Treat depression and anxiety
- Learn relaxation techniques
- Participate in cardiac rehabilitation programs
